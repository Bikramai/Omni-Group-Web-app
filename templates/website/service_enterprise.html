About Saif Enterprises

Omni Enterprises is a leading Elevator & Escalator Company headquartered in Uganda, the company since its inception in 2010, engaged in installing superior quality local and foreign branded elevators and escalators.

We also provide world-class installation services, repair services and maintenance services of elevator & escalator to residential and commercial customers.

Saif Enterprises import elevators and escalators from USA, China, and Turkey. Our Major Brands are Shanghai Fuji Elevator (China), Merih Elevator (Turkey), Otis (USA) and many other world-class elevators and escalators brands around the world.
